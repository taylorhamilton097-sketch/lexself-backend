Questions

Frequently asked

Is there really a free plan?
Yes — no credit card required. The free plan gives you 10 chat messages and 1 document analysis to see exactly what ClearStand can do before committing to a paid plan.
Can I switch between monthly and annual?
Yes. You can switch at any time from your account. Switching to annual is prorated — you're credited for unused days on your current billing cycle.
What's the difference between standalone and bundle?
Standalone plans cover one product — either Family Law or Criminal Defence. Bundle plans cover both at a discounted combined rate. If you have both legal matters, the bundle is always better value.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. Upgrades apply immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep full access until then.
What is the ClearSplit subscriber discount?
If you have an active ClearStand subscription (any paid plan), ClearSplit is available to you at $249 instead of $299 — automatically applied at checkout.
Is ClearStand a replacement for a lawyer?
No. ClearStand is a legal preparation tool. It helps you understand your situation, organize your case, and prepare documents — but it is not legal advice and does not create a lawyer-client relationship. For complex matters, consult a lawyer.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payments are processed securely — ClearStand never stores your card details.
What happens if I cancel?
You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that your account moves to the free tier — your data is never deleted. You can reactivate anytime.
