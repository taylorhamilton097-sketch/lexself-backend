Common Questions

Is ClearStand legal advice?
No. ClearStand is a legal preparation tool that helps you understand your situation, organize your case, and prepare documents. It is not a substitute for advice from a licensed lawyer. For serious matters, we always recommend consulting duty counsel or Legal Aid Ontario (1-800-668-8258).
Which provinces does ClearStand cover?
ClearStand Criminal Defence covers all of Canada under the Criminal Code. ClearStand Family Law is currently optimized for Ontario proceedings, including all Ontario family court forms and the Family Law Rules.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. You can cancel at any time through your account settings. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
What is the 5-pass Crown disclosure analysis?
When you upload your Crown disclosure package, ClearStand runs five separate AI analyses: narrative inconsistencies, Charter rights violations, witness credibility issues, missing disclosure items, and an overall defence strategy. The full report is exportable as a PDF.
What are the Counsel-only features?
Counsel includes four premium tools not available on other plans: voice dictation with AI language cleanup, the affidavit interview builder (which converts your spoken answers into court-ready numbered paragraphs with integrated case law), the cross-examination prep builder, and the defence organization tool.
Is my data secure?
Documents you upload are processed in memory only and are not stored on our servers. Your case profile is stored locally in your browser. We do not sell your data.
