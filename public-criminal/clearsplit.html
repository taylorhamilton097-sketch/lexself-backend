AI-Powered Valuation

Know what your property
is actually worth.

ClearSplit uses AI to analyze your property based on address, property type, square footage, year built, condition, and recent neighbourhood sales you describe. The result is an honest, realistic market value range — not an inflated number that creates conflict.

If both parties cannot agree on a value, ClearSplit displays a mandatory notice:

"Disputed property values must be resolved by a certified appraiser (AACI designation) before this agreement can be finalized. An agreed-upon value is required to proceed."

This protects both parties. Courts will not accept a separation agreement where property values are disputed without independent appraisal evidence.

Witness Requirement Ontario separation agreements require each signature to be witnessed. Your witness must be present at signing and cannot be the other spouse. ClearSplit recommends having signatures commissioned before a notary public or commissioner for oaths for added enforceability, though this is not legally required for a separation agreement.
